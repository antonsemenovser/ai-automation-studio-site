Appointment-booking bot: 24/7 scheduling & reminders

A booking bot lets clients see your real availability and book themselves, 24/7, syncs to your calendar, and sends reminders that cut no-shows. A self-booking bot starts around €150–€400; deposits + service/staff selection + multi-location lands €400–€900.

Last reviewed: June 2026.

What a booking bot does

  • Self-service booking. Clients pick a free slot in a few taps — at any hour.
  • Calendar sync. Reads real availability, writes confirmed bookings back; no double-booking.
  • Automatic reminders. Cuts no-shows with timely nudges before the appointment.
  • Reschedule & cancel. Clients change their slot themselves — no phone tag.
  • Deposits (optional). Take a deposit at booking to protect high-value slots.
  • Service & staff selection. Pick the service, person and location up front.
