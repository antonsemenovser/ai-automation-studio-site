Telegram bot for an online shop: catalog, cart & orders

A Telegram bot turns a chat into a storefront: catalog, cart, in-chat payment, order tracking and abandoned-cart nudges — no app install. A catalog-and-order bot starts around €150–€400; payments + automation + CRM sync lands €400–€1,200.

Last reviewed: June 2026.

What a shop bot does

  • Product catalog. Photos, prices, variants and categories — browsable in-chat.
  • Cart & checkout. Add items, choose options, check out in a few taps.
  • In-chat payment. Pay through a real provider without leaving Telegram.
  • Order confirmation & tracking. Automatic receipts and status updates.
  • Abandoned-cart & restock nudges. Win back buyers who hesitated.
  • Support. Answer sizing, shipping and return questions instantly.

Bot or full webstore?

It's not either/or. For impulse buys, repeat customers, flash sales and Telegram-heavy regions, a bot converts fast with zero install friction. Many shops run a bot alongside their website to capture chat-native buyers and re-engage customers right where they already chat.

Yes — Telegram in-chat payments through a real provider; funds arrive as normal.

Often yes — sync products/orders with an existing store or sheet; integration depends on your platform.
