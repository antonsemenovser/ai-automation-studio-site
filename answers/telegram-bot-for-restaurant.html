Telegram bot for a restaurant: menu, orders & bookings

A Telegram bot gives a restaurant or cafe a digital menu, table bookings, takeaway orders, loyalty and reminders — all in a chat customers already use, with no app to install. A simple build starts around €120–€300; ordering + payments + loyalty lands €300–€800.

Last reviewed: June 2026.

What a restaurant bot does

  • Always-fresh menu. Update prices and dishes once; no reprinting, no outdated PDF.
  • Table bookings. Customers reserve in a few taps; you get the booking instantly.
  • Takeaway & delivery orders. Browse, order and pay inside the chat; order lands in your kitchen channel.
  • Loyalty & offers. Stamp cards, discounts and "we miss you" nudges to bring people back.
  • Instant answers. Hours, location, allergens, parking — asked and answered without staff time.
  • QR on the table. A sticker opens the bot — menu and ordering in seconds.

Why a bot beats a restaurant app

Most diners won't download a separate app for one venue. A Telegram bot lives in an app they already have — open via link or QR, no install, no friction. You get the same ordering and loyalty features at a fraction of the cost and you reach customers where they already are.

FAQ

What can it do for a restaurant?
Digital menu, table bookings, takeaway/delivery orders, loyalty, reminders and instant answers — all in Telegram, no app install.
