AI document processing: extract data from invoices, PDFs & forms

AI document processing reads your invoices, receipts, PDFs and forms, pulls out the fields you need, and enters them into your systems automatically — no more manual typing. A focused extractor starts around €300–€1,000; multi-document with validation lands €1,000–€3,000.

Last reviewed: June 2026.

What it does, step by step

  1. Receives the document — emailed, uploaded, or dropped in a folder.
  2. Reads & understands it — even varied layouts, because it reads content not a fixed template.
  3. Extracts the fields — amounts, dates, names, line items, whatever you need.
  4. Validates — checks against rules; flags anything uncertain for a quick human glance.
  5. Writes it in — straight into your sheet, accounting tool, CRM or database.

What it can handle

  • Invoices & receipts — amounts, tax, dates, supplier, line items.
  • Forms & applications — intake, registration, onboarding.
  • Contracts & delivery notes — key terms and references.
  • PDFs and photos/scans — both work; varied quality handled with review.

How accurate is it?

On clear, consistent documents, a well-built system is highly accurate. For messy or low-quality scans, accuracy stays high with validation rules plus a human-review step for anything the AI is unsure about. The aim isn't blind 100% automation — it's removing the bulk of manual entry while flagging the few items worth a look.
