Lead-generation bot: how it captures & qualifies leads

A lead-generation bot greets every visitor, asks a few qualifying questions, captures their contact, and routes a warm lead to you instantly — 24/7, so nothing is lost. A capture-and-qualify bot starts around €120–€400; multi-channel + CRM + follow-ups lands €400–€1,000.

Last reviewed: June 2026.

How it works, step by step

  1. Greet. A visitor opens the bot from your site, ad or social link.
  2. Qualify. It asks budget, need, timing — the questions you'd ask anyway.
  3. Capture. It collects contact details and tags the lead.
  4. Route. You get the warm lead instantly, with the details attached.
  5. Follow up. It nudges leads who didn't finish, recovering lost deals.

What you get

  • No missed enquiries. It answers at 2am and during your busiest hour alike.
  • Warm leads only. Qualification filters out tyre-kickers before they reach you.
  • Source tracking. Tagged links show which channel actually converts.
  • Automatic follow-up. Timely nudges keep prospects from going cold.
