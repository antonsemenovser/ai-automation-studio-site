Telegram bot for real estate: listings, leads & viewings

A Telegram bot gives an agent or agency searchable listings, instant lead capture, viewing bookings and automatic new-listing alerts — so no enquiry is lost, even after hours. A listings-and-leads bot starts around €200–€500; bookings + alerts + qualification + CRM lands €500–€1,200.

Last reviewed: June 2026.

What a real-estate bot does

  • Searchable listings. Filter by price, rooms, area; photos and details in-chat.
  • Instant lead capture. Every enquiry is caught with contact + criteria, day or night.
  • Lead qualification. Asks budget, location, timing, buy-or-rent — hands you warm leads only.
  • Viewing bookings. Buyers pick a slot; you get the appointment instantly.
  • New-listing & price-drop alerts. Notifies matching buyers automatically.
  • Instant answers. Fees, documents, areas, process — without your time.

Why it wins deals

In real estate, the agent who responds first usually wins. A bot replies instantly, 24/7, captures the lead with full criteria, and qualifies it before you ever pick up the phone — so you spend time only on serious buyers and never lose one to a slow reply.
