How much does an AI chatbot cost in 2026?

Short answer: a custom AI chatbot trained on your own data starts around €300 for a freelance build, typically lands €300–€1,500 for a small business, and reaches €2,000–€10,000+ for enterprise-grade systems. Here's what drives the price and how to avoid overpaying.

Why an AI chatbot costs more than a scripted bot

A scripted bot follows fixed buttons and flows — cheap and predictable. An AI chatbot understands free-text questions and answers from your knowledge base. That extra value comes from real work:

  • Data preparation. Your FAQ, prices, policies and docs have to be cleaned and structured so the bot can use them.
  • Grounding (RAG). Retrieval keeps answers tied to your content instead of the model's imagination.
  • Anti-hallucination guardrails. When the answer isn't in your data, the bot must hand off to a human — not guess.
  • Multilingual handling. Answering each customer in their own language.
  • Testing. Running real questions through it to catch wrong or unsafe answers before launch.

Ongoing costs

  • Hosting: roughly €3–€10/month.
  • AI model usage: if the bot uses a paid LLM API, you pay per message — usually a fraction of a cent to a few cents each. Built on local or open models, per-message cost is near-zero.

For a small business, picking the right model is often the difference between a chatbot that costs cents a month and one that costs hundreds.

How to avoid overpaying

  1. Start with a tight knowledge base. A focused bot on your top 30 questions beats a sprawling one that's never accurate.
  2. Insist on grounding + handoff. "Answers only from your data, hands off otherwise" should be non-negotiable.
  3. Ask about per-message cost up front. A local/open-model build can save a lot at volume.
  4. Own the result. Get the source and run it under your own accounts.

FAQ

How much does an AI chatbot cost?
From around €300 for a fixed-scope freelance build; €600–€1,500 for a typical small business; €2,000–€10,000+ for enterprise.
Why is it more expensive than a scripted bot?
It understands free text and answers from your data, which needs data prep, retrieval/grounding, anti-hallucination guardrails and testing.
What are the ongoing costs?
Hosting ~€3–€10/month plus per-message AI-model cost if a paid model is used — near-zero on local/open models.
