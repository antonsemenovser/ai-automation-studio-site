How much does a custom Telegram bot cost in 2026?

Short answer: a simple Telegram bot costs roughly €35–€120, a business bot with lead capture and a mini-CRM €75–€250, and an AI chatbot trained on your own data starts around €300. Below: what drives the price, real market ranges, and how to avoid overpaying.

Last reviewed: June 2026 · based on freelance-marketplace rates and current build costs.

For reference, the average price of a Telegram-bot gig on freelance marketplaces in 2026 is around €120–€135, with basic bots near €45–€60 and advanced builds €500+. AI chatbots commonly land in the €200–€340 range for fixed-scope freelance work and far higher for enterprise.

What actually drives the price

  • Number of conversation flows. One lead form is cheap; a bot that takes orders, books slots, and answers FAQs is more.
  • Integrations. Connecting to a CRM, payment provider, scheduling tool or your website adds work.
  • Database & admin. Storing leads, plus owner commands like /leads and /stats or an admin panel.
  • Languages. A multilingual bot that replies in each customer's language is worth more than a single-language one.
  • AI features. A scripted bot is cheaper than one that understands free text with an AI model.
  • Hosting & support. Whether you need it hosted 24/7 and maintained, or just handed over.

Are there ongoing costs?

The Telegram Bot API is free. Running costs are:

  • Hosting: a small server or serverless runs roughly €3–€10/month.
  • AI/LLM usage (only if the bot uses a paid model): a per-message API cost. A bot built on local or open models runs at near-zero per message.

How to avoid overpaying

  1. Start with one flow. Launch the single bot that solves your biggest pain (usually lead capture), prove it, then extend.
  2. Get the source code. Make sure you own the bot and it runs under your account — not locked to the developer.
  3. Watch for a too-cheap quote. A €10 bot is usually a template with no support; a fair simple bot is €35–€120.
  4. Ask for a free scope first. A good builder will sketch the simplest setup that fits before quoting.
